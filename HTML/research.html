REFERENCES TAKEN WHILE BUILDING OUR WEBSITE

While building our website our team had to take references from other websites to make our website better. The screenshots and comparison of our websites and the websites we took references from are below

1) Reference taken while building the footer for our website

The above screenshots show the references we took from the Soft Benz Infosys company's website while building the footer for our website. The footer of our printer selling e-commerce website was inspired by the design and layout elements found in the footer of Soft Benz Infosys company's website, incorporating similar features and information to enhance user experience and functionality.

Similarities between the two pages:

1)The design and footprint of the footer is quite similar to the reference page
2)There is some kind of a form to fill in the footer of both websites

Differences between the two pages:

1)The components inside the footer of our website is different than the components inside the footer of the reference site.
2)There is chat box in the reference page wheras our page doesnt have a chatbox.

2) Reference taken whille creating a certain part in home page

By taking references from the Soft Benz Infosys company's website, in our home page we have used circular icons with images which tell about the features of our company like excellent tech support,leading tech,affordability,and 10 years of establishment.

Similarities between the two pages:

1)Both page has circular images side by side with text below which shows the information about the company.
2)Both page has a heading above the images.

Differences between the two pages:

1)Our page has an hover element in the circular images wheras the reference page doesn't.
2)Our page is a lot more colourful than the reference page.

3) Reference taken while building product page

While building the product page of our website, references from product page of Canon printers was taken.The products picture placement from canon.com was taken as the reference to make our products list.

Similarities between the two pages:

1) Both page has divisions containing printer's name,photo and price side by side.
2) Both page has a 1-5 star rating system for each printer.

Differences between the two pages:

1)In the reference page there is a big button for add to cart but our page just has an add to cart icon instead of a button.
2)The reference page has an AI chatbot wheras we dont.

4) Reference taken while building the about us page

While building the about us page of our website, reference from the Canon USA website was taken.Their page with image and text on the side was replicated in our webpage .

Similarities between the two pages:

1) Image on the left side and text on the right side of the picture.
2)Both webpages have a button or a link that leads to the products page of the website.

Differences between the two pages:

1) Our page is a lot more simpler and minimilistic than the page taken as reference.
2) Our color palette is a lot more lighter than the reference website.

4) Reference taken while building the blog page

While building the blog page of our website, reference from the Research Gate website was taken.

Similarities between the two pages:

1) Image on the top and text below it.

Differences between the two pages:

1) Our page doesnt have links in the left side of the page.
2) Our page has a heading on the right side.
